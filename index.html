RoboticonQuest

RoboticonQuest is a multiplayer strategy game, where the aim is to take over the University of York using your army of roboticons! Players take turns to take over land and acquire resources, battling it out to become the Vice-Chancellor of the colony. View the progress of the development of the game below.

The Team: Fractal

Fractals, like video games, can be considered a combination of Art, Maths and Computer Science. This makes them very appropriate for the basis of our team.

Fractals start off simple and gets more and more complex over time until something very intricate and aesthetically pleasing is made. We hope our project parallels this idea, starting off with simple but concrete design ideas, which over the course of many iterations transform into an immersive gaming experience.
